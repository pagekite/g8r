Usage: g8r host-info <host>

Print a human-readable summary of a single host: its FQDN, IPv4 and
IPv6 addresses, and the public bootstrap URL that a freshly-installed
VPS would curl|bash to provision itself.

<host> may be a short hostname, a FQDN, or the host's secret. If a
short name is ambiguous across domains, use the FQDN.

Usage: g8r backups -a
       g8r backups -d <domain>
       g8r backups -l <pattern>
       g8r backups <host1 host2 ... hostN>

Fetch the backups from one or more hosts (as identified by their host
secret) and update `tree/backups-manifest.json`.

The `-a`, `-d` and `-l` modes will act upon the same hosts as would be
listed by the equivalent `g8r hosts` command.
